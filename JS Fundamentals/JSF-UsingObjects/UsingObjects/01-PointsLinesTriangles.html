01. Write functions for working with shapes in standard Planar coordinate system
    - Points are represented by coordinates P(X, Y)
    - Lines are represented by two points, marking their beginning and ending
    L(P1(X1,Y1), P2(X2,Y2))
    - Calculate the distance between two points
    - Check if three segment lines can form a triangle

Add coordinates for the starting and ending point of a line and click 'Add and Execute!'.
You will get the distance between those two points and once you've entered more than 3 lines the page will check if the last three can form a triangle.
If you want to start with a new empty set of lines refresh the page.
